What's our story?

We're bread people. And we love bread! But we hate the ends. Our goal was to provide a delicious tasing bread that only contained the pieces people love to eat.

We decided the best way to do this was to remove the end pieces from every loaf.

Now you don't have to fight over who eats the end pieces - everyone can enjoy a full slice of bread.

Since we don't charge more for our product you get more bread for your money.
